NewEdo XP Calculator

Here are some ways you can spend XP in NewEdo!

Increase Attributes

Attributes are your core stats, i.e. Heart, Power, Reflex, etc.. The calculation is 2 XP times the Rank of the attribute per point of increase.

Learn or Improve Skills

The cost of improving or learning a skill depends on the current Rank you have in the skill (i.e. how many dice you have added to your dice pool for that skill, excluding bonuses from paths and cybernetics) and the size of the die you want to add.

To use the calculator, enter your current Rank in the skill (if you haven't learned the skill yet, enter 0) and select the die you want to add, then click the Calculate button.

Learn Rotes

Every rote costs 10 XP regardless of what Kami it comes from. You may only learn rotes from Kami that you already have access to, and you may not buy access to other Kami with XP.

If you want to gain access to a kami that you normally would not be able to access, ask the GM how you might do so.

Improve Backgrounds

Each point gained in a background costs 2 XP regardless of your current Rank in the background, but you may only put a maximum of 3 points into backgrounds per session.

Change your Fate

You can increase specific lines of your fate card by spending 3 XP per 1% increase, but you may only do so up to two times per session. You may not change your crit chance this way.
